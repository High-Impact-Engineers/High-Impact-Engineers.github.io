__Helping engineers do high impact work.__

The world is full of important and pressing problems and engineers are often very well placed to address these challenges.

High Impact Engineers is a community of engineers who want to maximise their positive impact on the world.

If you want to join the community, check out our forum, [the Impact Forge](https://github.com/orgs/High-Impact-Engineers/discussions), to discuss ideas, ask for advice, share your projects, and more!
